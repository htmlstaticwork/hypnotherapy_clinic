Still Hesitant?

Will I lose control or share secrets?

Absolutely not. Hypnosis is a state of hyper-focus, not unconsciousness. You are in complete control of your speech and actions throughout the entire protocol.

What if I can't be hypnotized?

Hypnosis is a natural human ability. If you can daydream or get lost in a movie, you can enter a hypnotic state. We simply guide the process professionally.

How many sessions do I need?

On average, significant shifts occur within 3–6 sessions. However, the diagnostic call will provide a more accurate estimate for your unique needs.
